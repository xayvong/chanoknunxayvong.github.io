About Me
Chanoknun Xayvong

Hi, my name is Chanoknun Xayvong, but I go by Cody for short. I started my education for software technology sometime in 2015 and have been going to school on and off. I opened a restaurant in 2017 called New Sun and ran a successful restaurant for almost 5 years. I tried to continue my schooling, but eventually the restaurant became too busy and I had to focus my efforts there. After almost 5 years of running a successful restaurant, I decided that this isn't what I wanted to do with my life and sold my business to finish my education in Software Technology.

I've always been passionate about technology and even dreamed of creating my own games when I was a child. This passion lead me to pursure an education in Software Technology so that I could achieve that goal. I've realized throughout my life that passion about something not only helps you achieve growth, but it also gives you a reason to strive to improve and better yourself. I'm passionate about martial arts, and I'm a 4th degree blackbelt in Taekwondo that has been practicing and teaching it for 15+ years now. I'm also passionate about music and have been creating and producing music for the same amount of time. The skills I have aquired through my hobbies directly influence my ability to solve problems and learn new things, which I will carry with me for any goals I try to achieve and pursue.
